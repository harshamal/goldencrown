Sri Lanka Heritage Journey

An 8-day odyssey through ancient ruins, wildlife, and scenic landscapes.

Heritage Journey Itinerary

Embark on a cultural odyssey through Sri Lanka’s ancient ruins, wildlife, and scenic beauty.

Day 1: Pinnawala & Sigiriya Rock

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport, meet your guide, and visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Travel to Sigiriya, check into your hotel, and visit the Sigiriya Rock Fortress.

Day 2: Polonnaruwa

Breakfast at the hotel, travel to Polonnaruwa to visit cultural sites, including ancient palaces and temples. Afternoon jeep safari in Minneriya National Park to see elephants and wildlife.

Day 3: Golden Rock Temple & Temple of the Tooth

Travel to Kandy via Dambulla, visit the Golden Rock Temple’s frescoes, and the Nalanda Gedige temple. In Kandy, visit the Temple of the Tooth and enjoy a cultural dance.

Day 4: Tea Factory & Ramboda Waterfall

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, visit a tea factory and Ramboda Waterfall. Enjoy a city tour and visit Gregory Lake in the evening.

Day 5: Ella Gap & Ravana Waterfall

Travel to Yala, visit Ella Gap and Ravana Waterfall. Evening game drive in Yala National Park to spot wildlife.

Day 6: Rathnapura Gem Mine

Travel to Colombo via Rathnapura, visit the gem mines, and explore the Gem Museum.

Day 7: Colombo & Negombo

City tour in Colombo, then travel to Negombo for a beach vacation and seafood dining.

Day 8: Depart

Depart in good time for your flight home from the airport.

Sri Lanka Coastal Adventure

A 7-day journey blending beaches, culture, and highland treks.

Coastal Adventure Itinerary

Experience pristine beaches, thrilling treks, and cultural landmarks along Sri Lanka’s coast.

Day 1: Colombo

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport, meet your guide, and transfer to a hotel in Colombo. Rest of the day at leisure to explore the city.

Day 2: Elephant Orphanage & Temple of the Tooth

Travel to Kandy, visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, and explore the Temple of the Tooth with a cultural dance in the evening.

Day 3: Tea Factory & Ramboda Waterfall

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, visit a tea factory and Ramboda Waterfall, and enjoy a city tour including Gregory Lake.

Day 4: Horton Plains & Kalani River

Early morning hike in Horton Plains, then travel to Kitulgala for a visit to the Kelani River, site of "Bridge on the River Kwai."

Day 5: Beach Vacation

Travel to Weligama for a beach vacation, enjoy strolling on the golden beach and water activities.

Day 6: Enjoy Your Beach Vacation

Continue your beach stay with water skiing, wind surfing, or relaxing with tropical drinks.

Day 7: Depart

Tour Galle Dutch Fortress and Colombo, then transfer to the airport for departure.
