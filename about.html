Our Story

From humble beginnings to a global leader, our journey is one of passion and purpose.

2015: Founded

Crown Global Services was established in Sri Lanka with a vision to connect people with global opportunities.

2017: SLBFE License

Received SLBFE License No: 3065, solidifying our commitment to ethical recruitment.

2020: Expanded Services

Launched travel and education divisions, offering holistic global solutions.

2025: Global Impact

Serving thousands of clients across travel, recruitment, and education worldwide.
