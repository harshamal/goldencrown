Legendary Destinations

Ten Places That Stay With You Forever

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ascend this UNESCO World Heritage Site, adorned with ancient frescoes and offering breathtaking views.

Mirissa Beach

Surf, swim and savor sunsets on this vibrant southern coast, a haven for beach lovers.

Yala National Park

Embark on thrilling safaris to spot leopards, elephants and exotic wildlife.

Kandy

Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth and experience the vibrant Esala Perahera festival.

Ella

Hike through tea plantations and marvel at the scenic Nine Arch Bridge.

Nuwara Eliya

Stroll through cool tea country, colonial bungalows and botanical gardens in Little England.

Anuradhapura

Explore ancient stupas, sacred Bo tree shrines and monastic ruins thousands of years old.

Dambulla Cave Temple

Marvel at colorful frescoes and Buddha statues inside sacred rock caves.

Polonnaruwa

Walk through ancient royal palaces, temples and beautifully carved statues from the medieval capital.

Trincomalee

Relax on unspoiled beaches or dive into marine wonders off the northeast coast.

Living Traditions

Sri Lanka's Soulful Heritage

Esala Perahera

Witness the grand Kandy festival, where elephants parade with sacred relics in a vibrant spectacle.

Ayurvedic Wellness

Rejuvenate with ancient healing practices at wellness retreats across the island.

Traditional Crafts

Learn batik, mask making or pottery from skilled artisans in rural villages.

Sri Lankan Cuisine

Savor spicy curries, string hoppers and kottu roti in bustling local eateries.

Kandyan Dance

Marvel at traditional dance performances rich in rhythm and ritual attire.

Temple Architecture

Explore ancient dagobas and rock carved temples that echo timeless devotion.

Vesak Festival

Celebrate the island wide Buddhist festival with illuminated pandals and acts of generosity.

Gem Crafting

Discover the art of gem cutting and jewelry making in Ratnapura's gem markets.
